About Us

The Legacy of Indian Weavers

Aswathi Textiles carries forward the rich legacy of Indian weavers, crafting fine fabrics from threads woven in renowned mills.

  • Handpicked fabrics from the finest weaving locations across India.
  • Crafted with passion, combining tradition with modern techniques.
  • Fabrics sourced from hidden gems like Erode, Kollengode, Koduvayur, and Kuthampully.

We are dedicated to preserving the essence of traditional weaving, bringing the finest Indian fabrics to the modern wardrobe.
